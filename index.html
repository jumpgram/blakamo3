Is Jumpgram safe?

Of course! At Jumpgram, above all else, the safety of your account is our #1 priority. We do our best by making sure we never exceed Instagram's rate limits when performing interactions on your account. Don't worry, we've been doing this for a long time!

How fast will my account grow & how?

Growth ranges from ~1-10k followers per month. Growth speed is dependent on various factors, such as your quality of content, consistency and niche. This is why we can't guarantee a specific number of followers.

Will Jumpgram work for me & are my followers actually real?

Jumpgram can absolutely work for anyone! We know how frustrating it can be to have followers that aren't engaged with your content, or don't seem like real people. This is why we take targeting seriously! All followers are 100% REAL!

How do I know you'll attract people that are interested in my profile?

At Jumpgram, we're big on providing a quality service - inside and out. We aren't just satisfied with growing your account. Quality and relevance of followers is super important to us! When signing up, you'll specify which type of accounts you'd like us to engage with, this is to make sure your account gets followers that make sense! Not only can you customise who you target, but you can change this at any time!

Can I cancel any time?

Yes! You can cancel your subscription any time if you don't love Jumpgram's results (without being charged if done during your 7-day trial*). Your followers will continue following you & engaging with your content if you do decide to cancel, since they were gained organically.
